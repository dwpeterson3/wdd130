Purpose

Have you ever wanted to experience a trip of a lifetime? We wish to provide wonderful experiences and a thrilling time for adventure seekers

Audience

What do they want? They want to have an enjoyable water experience with some adrenaline mixed in. Who are the target customers? Our target audience will be anyone from families wanting to spend time with each other to experienced white water rafters coming for an adventure. All are welcome in the rafts. What do they expect to find or what question will be answered by them accessing the website? I expect to tell them prices and different options from leisure sight-seeing trips to thrilling adrenaline filled trips.
